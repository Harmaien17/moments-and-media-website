WHAT IS 3D ANIMATION?

3D animation is the process of creating moving 
images in a three-dimensional (3D) environment, 
giving the illusion that these digital objects are 
moving through a 3D space. This is done by using 
computer software to create and manipulate 
digital objects, or by using specialized hardware.

ADVANTAGES OF USING 3D ANIMATION
FOR ADVERTISEMENTS

Development of a realistic picture:
3D animations can create a realistic picture
of the product or service being advertised,
allowing for a better understanding of its
attributes and features.

Visual effects:
3D animations can bring the attributes of
the product or service to the surface,
emphasizing them with high-quality
visual effects.

Cost-effective and flexible:
3D animations can be produced quickly
and at a lower cost compared to traditional
methods, and are highly flexible allowing
for the creation of a virtual world.

Brand identity:
3D animations can be used to create a
distinct brand identity, setting a business
apart from its competitors and making it more
memorable.

Increased engagement:
3D animations can increase engagement and
interaction with the advertisement, leading
to a higher return on investment (ROI) for
businesses and are highly attention grabbing.
